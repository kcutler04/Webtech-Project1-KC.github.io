DIY Tie Dye

Tie dye is a fun project that is like unwrapping a present when seeing finished product. The finished product is always exciting.

Supplies needed

  • Plain white Tshirt
  • Choice of dye colors
  • Rubber bands or string
  • Disposable gloves
  • Large ziplock bag or shopping bag
  • Garbage bag or plastic table cloth. Use this to protect the surface so you don't dye anything on accident.

Pick your design. My favorite go to is a simple swirl. To achieve this pinch the center of the shirt and twist clockwise until you form a circle.

Instuctions:

  1. Fold or twist for your design.
  2. Slip on rubber bands start by center of shirt then oposite like cutting a pie. make sure you keep shirt as flat as you can.
  3. Apply your dye working one section at a time.
    (hint apply lighter colors first.)
  4. Flip shirt over and repeat on opposite side. Try to use same color as other side
  5. Place your creation in ziplock back or shopping bag and WAIT.

Ready to see your creation?

After you have let your shirt sit for 24 hours you are ready to see the masterpiece. Put your gloves back on! In a sink remove from bag and start to remove your rubberbands or string. With cold water running slowly rinse the dye out. Once most of the dye is rinsed toss in washer and wash on cold.
